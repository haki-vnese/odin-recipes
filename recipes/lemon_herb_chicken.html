Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Description

This lemon herb grilled chicken is juicy, flavorful, and perfect for a quick dinner. The marinade combines fresh lemon juice, garlic, and herbs to create a bright and refreshing taste.

It pairs well with rice, salad, or roasted vegetables and works great for meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill or pan to medium heat.
  4. Cook chicken for 6â€“7 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  5. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
