Creamy Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Description

Creamy garlic shrimp pasta is a rich and comforting dish that feels restaurant-quality but is easy to make at home.

Tender shrimp are cooked in a buttery garlic cream sauce and tossed with pasta for a smooth and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (fettuccine or spaghetti)
  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions and drain.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Pour in cream and simmer for 3–4 minutes.
  5. Add cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Season and top with Parmesan before serving.
