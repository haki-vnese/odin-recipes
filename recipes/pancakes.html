Fluffy Pancakes

Description

These homemade pancakes are soft, fluffy, and perfect for breakfast. They come together quickly using simple pantry ingredients.

Serve them warm with maple syrup, butter, fruit, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
